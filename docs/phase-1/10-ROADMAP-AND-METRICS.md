# Roadmap and metrics

Phase 2: design system and complete public site. Phase 3: Supabase, roles, lead pipeline, invitations, email. Phase 4: projects, milestones, tasks, files, messages, approvals. Phase 5: proposals, Stripe deposits, invoices, subscriptions. Phase 6: tests, accessibility, performance, security, monitoring, analytics, legal review.

Track qualified inquiries, form completion, case-study engagement, service-page engagement, qualified lead rate, discovery-to-proposal, proposal acceptance, average project value, project margin, approval turnaround, and care-plan attachment.

Working targets: three complete case studies; at least half of inquiries meet budget floor; 25% of qualified discovery calls become proposals; 40% of proposals become projects; 30% of launched clients choose care plans. These are internal targets, not promises.
