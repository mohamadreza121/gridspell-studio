# Case study plan

Launch with three strong studies.

## DESA Foam Insulation

Real business website, service architecture, project gallery, estimate workflow, Google review integration. Collect before/after screens, production performance, form workflow, client feedback, and real inquiry results over time.

## Pure Timepieces

Luxury brand, product storytelling, authentication, inventory/account/payment foundations. Show art direction, product detail, mobile, trust flow, and system architecture.

## GridSpell Client OS

Internal portal/admin product demonstrating roles, database design, lead pipeline, milestones, approvals, files, messages, proposals, and billing.

Never invent results. Use measurable implementation outcomes until business results are available.
