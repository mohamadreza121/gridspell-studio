# GridSpell Phase 1 — Strategy and Content Foundation

Phase 1 defines what GridSpell sells, who it serves, how it is positioned, what the website communicates, and what the first product version contains.

## Decisions

- Brand: **GridSpell**
- Business: Independent premium web design and full-stack development studio
- Location: Toronto, Ontario, serving clients remotely
- Tagline: **Built on structure. Designed to captivate.**
- Primary audience: Established service businesses that have outgrown a basic website
- Secondary audiences: Premium product businesses, professional firms, and founders needing portals or dashboards
- Primary offer: Strategy-led business websites and redesigns
- Expansion offer: Client portals, dashboards, and full-stack applications
- Commercial model: Fixed-scope projects plus monthly care plans
- Visual direction: Obsidian surfaces, violet-to-cyan highlights, editorial typography, structured depth, restrained motion
- Authentication: Invitation-only client accounts
- Stack: Next.js + Supabase

Read the numbered documents in this folder before changing the product scope.
