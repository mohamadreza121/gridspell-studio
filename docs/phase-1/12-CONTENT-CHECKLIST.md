# Content and asset checklist

Brand: final logo/wordmark/icons, social image, domain, email, profiles.

Founder: professional portrait, short bio, relevant experience, approach, Toronto/remote statement.

Each case study: permission, client logo, industry, dates, problem, scope, before screens, desktop/mobile finals, features, technology, measured results, testimonial.

Each service: final name, starting price, timeline, deliverables, exclusions, relevant work, FAQs.

Trust: testimonials, permitted logos, performance measurements, process visuals, reviewed privacy/terms, contract and proposal templates.

Sales: qualification rules, discovery questions, proposal validity, deposits, revisions, cancellation, content delays, acceptance, care plans.
