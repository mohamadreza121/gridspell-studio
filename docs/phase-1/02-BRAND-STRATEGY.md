# Brand strategy

## Meaning

**Grid** represents structure, systems, precision, hierarchy, engineering, and responsive layout. **Spell** represents attraction, emotion, storytelling, movement, and memorability.

## Positioning

GridSpell is a Toronto web design and full-stack development studio creating premium business websites, client portals, dashboards, and digital systems for ambitious companies that have outgrown templates and disconnected tools.

## Promise

Make the client's business feel more credible, more valuable, and easier to engage with—without sacrificing technical quality.

## Personality

Confident, creative, technical, premium, direct, and detailed—never arrogant, chaotic, jargon-heavy, pretentious, cold, or bloated.

## Visual system

- Background `#07080C`
- Soft background `#0B0D13`
- Surface `#10131B`
- Raised surface `#161A24`
- Primary text `#F6F7FB`
- Secondary text `#A8ADBD`
- Violet `#7C5CFF`
- Cyan `#29D6FF`
- Soft cyan `#8BE9FF`

Use technical grids, fine borders, layered surfaces, editorial type, browser/dashboard frames, controlled asymmetry, and restrained glow. Motion must support hierarchy, remain fast, simplify on mobile, and respect reduced motion.
