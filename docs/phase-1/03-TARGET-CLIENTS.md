# Target clients

## Primary

Established local and professional service businesses: contractors, clinics, consultants, specialty automotive, property services, and B2B technical companies.

Common problems:

- Website looks old or generic
- Services are hard to understand
- Weak mobile experience and forms
- Competitors look more credible
- Reviews, booking, content, and CRM are disconnected
- Business has outgrown a template

## Secondary

Premium product and specialist businesses needing art direction, high-trust storytelling, and refined inquiry or commerce.

## Expansion

Founders and operations teams replacing spreadsheets, email chains, manual follow-up, and disconnected tools with a portal or dashboard.

## Poor fit

Very low budget, no decision maker, guaranteed ranking demands, unlimited revisions, copy requests with no strategy, or unrealistic deadlines without scope reduction.
